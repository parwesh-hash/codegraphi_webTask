AEPS Services

Empower your business with Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS) for instant cash withdrawals, balance enquiry, and deposits. Seamless integration ensures fast, secure, and reliable transactions for your customers.

DMT (Domestic Money Transfer)

Facilitate instant fund transfers across all major banks in India. Our B2B panel supports NEFT, IMPS, and RTGS, allowing businesses to manage bulk disbursements efficiently with full compliance and real-time tracking.

BBPS (Bill Payments)

Offer your customers a one-stop solution for utility bill payments including electricity, water, gas, and DTH. Revamoney ensures instant payment processing, easy reconciliation, and a robust backend for smooth B2B operations.

Insurance & Loans

Expand your business offerings with easy access to insurance and loan products. Revamoney B2B panel allows instant policy issuance, premium collection, and loan disbursal, helping service providers generate additional revenue streams effortlessly.
