AEPS Services

Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS) is a bank-led model that allows online interoperable financial transactions at PoS (Point of Sale / Micro ATM) through the Business Correspondent.

Our AEPS services enable you to offer cash withdrawal, balance enquiry, and mini statement services to your customers using just their Aadhaar number and biometric authentication.

Key Features:

  • Secure biometric authentication
  • Instant cash withdrawal
  • Balance enquiry and mini statements
  • Interoperable across all banks
  • 24/7 availability
  • Real-time transaction reporting

Commission on Transactions

BBPS Services

Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS) is a centralized bill payment system that offers interoperable and accessible bill payment service to customers through a network of agents.

Our BBPS platform enables you to offer multiple bill payment services including electricity, water, gas, telecom, DTH, insurance premiums, and loan repayments.

Key Features:

  • Wide range of bill categories
  • Instant payment confirmation
  • Multiple payment options
  • 24/7 service availability
  • Attractive commissions
  • Detailed reports and analytics
